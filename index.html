Our Organizational Profile

ReproClimate Alliance community based organization was founded on the 20th of February 2023 by a group of Kenyatta university Environmental Health students and lecturers from The Catholic University Of Eastern Africa, department of community health who came together with the objective of improving and upgrading the livelihoods of communities in the Kisumu county. They also realized the necessity of this establishment as a means of contributing to development and meeting communities’ needs which has been the driving force and motivation. Another motivating factor to the establishment of ReproClimate Alliance community based organization is the interest that agencies and other international humanitarian institutions operating in Kisumu county had similar objectives to support projects with similar objectives as ReproClimate Alliance. in order to create participation in development at community levels led to the establishment of ReproClimate Alliance community based organization. ReproClimate Alliance community based organization has also decided to join the team of Visionaries yearning to impact and develop Kisumu county. ReproClimate Alliance community based organization like other community based organizations, Non-Governmental Organizations and International Humanitarian institutions, we aim at achieving sustainable results from developmental activities and initiatives to our communities, especially with the youth, children, women. Many regions have seen deteriorations in many areas especially at community levels. Also there have been many instances of concern where humanitarian activities haven’t been implemented as projected. Some community based organizations, NGOs and even the Government authorities who have promised to bring certain developments, improvements or interventions have in many cases failed the people of Kisumu county and this goes a long way to affect the development of the region. However there are also many community Foundations and NGOs that strive to bring development and touch lives with great impact. These organizations have great passion and are diligent in intervening to provide development. The hindering factor to their ability to be able to effectively have impact through intervention projects has been crippled by the lack of funds or adequate funds in some cases. One of these sincere organizations looking out to make a difference and great impact is ReproClimate Alliance community based organization.

Goal

Our goal is to have IMPACT IN LIVES by providing high impact oriented, cost effective, & culturally competent capacity building programs to people in Kisumu county particularly.

Our Mission

ReproClimate Alliance community based organization is out to improve and upgrade communities, and enrich the lives of the community with purpose through community based advocacy programmes and trainings like sexual and reproductive health rights and sanitation awareness, and advancing climate resilient communities through climate literacy and advocacy.

Our Vision

Our vision is to see people within communities of Kisumu county living in a hygienic, friendly and healthy environment with the awareness of Sexual and reproductive health rights and environmental protection and conservation.

Improving the quality of life and poverty alleviation within the community

Ministries

We engage with local authorities to align our initiatives with community needs. Together, we develop programs that address youth unemployment, health, and infrastructure.

Local Communities

Community partnerships foster long-term sustainability by building local ownership and commitment.They hold valuable knowledge, cultural heritage, and social networks.

>Key Focus Areas

Development: We offer career guidance, leadership training, and personal development workshops. Our goal is to nurture well-rounded individuals who can contribute positively to their communities.

Partnerships: We collaborate with local government bodies, ministries, and other organizations to create a holistic ecosystem of support. By working together, we can address systemic challenges and amplify our impact.

Engagement: We actively involve young people in community service, volunteering, and social projects. Through these activities, we foster a sense of responsibility and civic pride.

ReproClimate Alliance community based organization is guided by and believes in the following core values:
Integrity and Accountability: The organization shall conduct all her work with utmost honesty and transparency. We shall not only do whatever is in our means to enhance good conduct in all our interactions at project and communication level but also accept responsibility for our actions.
Excellence: ReproClimate Alliance shall always promote project/program result-oriented hard work amongst staff that not only gives credit to its human resource but also contributes greatly in the achievement of her goals and strategic objectives.
Respect and Equal Treatment: We shall promote equal treatment for all people irrespective of age, sex, level of education, religious and political affiliation as well as socio-economic status. We shall also uphold the dignity, value contributions and potential of every individual. 
Timeliness: ReproClimate Alliance shall ensure that all her deliberations, projects, programs and reporting be done within the stipulated time frame.
